Lacovia Girls Football

The Lacovia High School Girls Football team keeps performing and getting better every year. Last year they qualified for the quater-finals against central high. Unfortunatley our Girls ended up losing that game but we still continue to give them our full support. About Us

Lacovia highschool, Founded in 1970, when the Hon. Edwin Allen was minister of education the then Lacovia Junior Secondary, was built by the Jamaican government on eight acres of land to accommodate 650 students from Lacovia and surrounding communities.
